Hello, I am John Michael

I am a passionate and dedicated 4th-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) student at STI College Koronadal. With a strong foundation in software development, web technologies, mobile applications, and IoT, I thrive on solving real-world problems through innovative solutions.

My expertise includes designing responsive websites, developing Android applications, and creating embedded systems that integrate IoT solutions. I enjoy working on full-stack projects, combining front-end and back-end technologies to build seamless digital experiences.

With a continuous learning mindset, I stay updated with the latest tech trends and best practices. I am eager to collaborate on exciting projects that push the boundaries of technology and make a meaningful impact.

Code is an art form, and every project is a masterpiece.
